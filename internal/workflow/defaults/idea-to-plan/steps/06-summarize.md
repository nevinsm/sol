# Summarize

Produce a final summary of the planning outcome.

Include:
1. **Original idea**: the input that started this workflow
2. **Interpretation**: how the idea was understood
3. **Design**: the chosen approach and why
4. **Work items**: list each created work item with its ID and title
5. **Risks or notes**: anything the operator should know

This summary serves as the record of what was planned and why. It should be
concise enough to scan quickly but complete enough to understand the decisions.

When the summary is complete, signal completion: `sol resolve`

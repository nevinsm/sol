# Understand the Intent

Parse the idea and clarify what is being asked:

> {{idea}}

1. Restate the idea in your own words — what outcome is the operator seeking?
2. Identify the core capability or behavior change requested
3. Note any implicit constraints or assumptions in the phrasing
4. List open questions — things the idea leaves ambiguous or unspecified

Make reasonable assumptions for ambiguities based on the codebase and existing
patterns. Document your assumptions explicitly so the operator can correct them
if needed.

When you have a clear understanding of what the idea means, advance:
`sol workflow advance --world={{world}} --agent=$SOL_AGENT`

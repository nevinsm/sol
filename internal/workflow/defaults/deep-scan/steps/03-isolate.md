# Isolate Root Cause

Narrow down to the specific root cause(s) using your survey knowledge.

1. Trace the bug path from trigger to symptom
2. Identify the exact lines and conditions that produce the behavior
3. Check for multiple contributing causes — there may be more than one
4. Distinguish between root causes and symptoms of deeper issues
5. Verify your theory explains all observed behavior, not just part of it

When you can explain exactly why the bug happens, advance:
`sol workflow advance --world=$SOL_WORLD --agent=$SOL_AGENT`
